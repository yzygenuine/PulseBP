Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PulseBP.

General

What is PulseBP?

PulseBP is a wellness app that helps you record and track your blood pressure and heart rate, visualize trends, set reminders, and export your records for sharing with healthcare professionals or family.

PulseBP is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice.
How does PulseBP work?

You manually record measurements in the app. PulseBP stores them locally on your device, generates charts and insights, and lets you set reminders for measurements or medications.

Privacy & Safety

Is my data safe with PulseBP?

Yes. Your health data (blood pressure and heart rate) is stored locally on your device by default. We do not upload your health data to our servers unless you choose to export or share it.

Do you show ads or use third‑party analytics?

No, PulseBP does not use in‑app advertising or third‑party analytics by default.

What personal information do you collect?

We do not require an account to use PulseBP. We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you directly contact us. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

Features

What can I track in PulseBP?
  • Blood pressure (systolic/diastolic)
  • Heart rate
  • Notes and context for each measurement
  • Trends and summaries over time
Can I export my data?

Yes. You can export your records to share with healthcare professionals or family. After export, handling of the exported file is your responsibility.

Can I set reminders?

Yes. You can set reminders for measurements or medications. Notifications are local and only used for reminders you enable.

Permissions

What permissions does the app need?
  • Notifications: Optional. Used to deliver reminders you set. You can change this anytime in your device settings.

Troubleshooting

Reminders are not appearing
  • Ensure notifications are allowed for PulseBP in system settings.
  • Check that the reminder time and frequency are set correctly.
  • Make sure your device is not in Do Not Disturb or Focus modes that suppress notifications.
I lost my data after deleting/reinstalling the app

Data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app may remove locally stored data. We recommend exporting your records regularly for backup.

How do I export my data?

Open the export feature in the app and choose your preferred format or destination. After exporting, keep your file secure and share it only with trusted parties.
